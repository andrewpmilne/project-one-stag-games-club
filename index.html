Events

Wednesday night games

The regular session playing a range of modern and classic board and card games in a friendly, relaxed setting.

These events take place every Wednesday (7:00pm - 10:15pm).

Fill in the contact form or check the group facebook page for information.

Saturday XL Games Day

Want to play games all day? Here is your chance! From 10:00am to 10:00pm we play a range of more complex, lengthy games. But there are still options for fun, quick party games too!

  • Saturday February 22th (10:00am - 10:00pm)
  • Saturday April 26th (10:00am - 10:00pm)
  • Saturday July 19th (10:00am - 10:00pm)
  • Saturday November 2nd (10:00am - 10:00pm)
FAWNS family games afternoon

Want to get your little ones into gaming? Here is your chance! With a whole range of lighter, family games availble, these events are free to anyone with children of any age! They take place on Sunday afternoons throughout the year.

  • Sunday 26th January (2:00pm - 5:00pm)
  • Sunday 23rd March (2:00pm - 5:00pm)
  • Sunday 29th June (2:00pm - 5:00pm)
  • Sunday 31st August (2:00pm - 5:00pm)
  • Sunday 2nd November (2:00pm - 5:00pm)
Christmas Gaming!

Meeting up on a Wednesday can sometimes get tricky around Christmas, but we offer an extra, 'tween-friendly' session in the period between Christmas and New Year.

  • Monday 29th December (7:00pm - 10:15pm)
